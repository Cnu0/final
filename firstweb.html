HU offers

HU offers undergraduate programs, Masters programs, and PhD programs.

Undergraduate Programs:

offers a wide range of undergraduate programs across different faculties and departments. These programs typically span four to five years, depending on the specific field of study. Students can earn bachelor's degrees in disciplines such as engineering, health sciences, business, agriculture, social sciences, natural sciences, and more.

Master's Programs

rovides master's programs for students who wish to further enhance their knowledge and expertise in specific fields. These programs usually require two years of study and research. Students can pursue master's degrees in areas such as engineering, health sciences, business administration, economics, social sciences, and natural sciences.

PhD Programs

offers PhD programs for those seeking in-depth research and advanced academic qualifications. These programs are typically designed for individuals who have already completed a master's degree in a related field. PhD programs at Hawassa University involve extensive research and the completion of a doctoral dissertation.
